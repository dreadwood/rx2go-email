How It Works

 
Step 1
 

ORDER

 
Easily input your customer's prescription details and delivery instructions with just a few clicks by using our intuitive dashboard. Alternatively, you can seamlessly import their data by connecting to our API.
 
 
Step 2
 

ATTACH THE LABEL

 
Print a personalized label and secure it on the package. You'll have access to all orders through the dashboard, complete with tracking features and proof of delivery details.
 
Step 3
 

HAND-OFF TO DRIVER

 
Upon collecting the packages, the driver scans them. This instantly confirms the pickup as complete by changing the order's status on both the dashboard and app.
 
 
Step 4
 

MONITOR DELIVERY

 
As our drivers advance, patients receive automated updates regarding their delivery status. Meanwhile, you can enjoy a convenient overview of all your deliveries.
 
 
Step 5
 

FINALIZE DELIVERY

 
Drivers conclude deliveries by scanning each item and uploading photos and signatures as evidence of completion. Patients are then invited to provide feedback while our quality assurance team ensures it was a success.

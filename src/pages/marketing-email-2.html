Next day service 8am-9am

The order will be picked up one day prior to the scheduled delivery date. It will be delivered between 8am-9pm. The patient will receive a text message in the morning with a precise 3-hour ETA.

9am-1pm 9am-3pm 2pm-5pm 3pm-9pm 5pm-9pm

Same day delivery

Orders created and picked up on the same day by 12:30 PM will be promptly delivered within the same day, between the hours of 3pm-9pm.

Stat delivery

Emergency delivery. The order will be delivered within 3 hours of receiving a request.

Real time text message notifications

Your patients can track and manage the delivery of their prescriptions. They will be able to change the delivery address, time, date and special instructions in real time.

Upon delivery, the patient will receive a confirmation with proof of delivery (signature and photo of the package).
